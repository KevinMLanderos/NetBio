CdeCMx Challenge 2020, Track C

What is it?

A call to learn and use new tools and scientific skills, mentored by instructors and collaborating as a team with other clubbers.

Vision

Open an online collaboration space where high school and college students, guided by instructors from the US and Mexico, can learn and develop problem solving skills using programming, data science, machine learning and their intersection with other areas of knowledge.

Our mision

Learn to integrate knowledge from various scientific areas for the development of antiviral therapies. We will study different options to develop antiviral therapy, such as analyzing interactions between proteins and modeling drugs that act as antivirals.

Programming

We implemented network construction using networkX in python.

SARS-CoV2 drug targets

We identified new drug targets based on biological interactions.

Networks

We used a network biology approach to manage this challenge.
